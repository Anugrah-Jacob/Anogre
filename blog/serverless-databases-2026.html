Serverless Databases in 2026

By Anugrah Jacob • April 27, 2026 • 6 min read

Managing database clusters is a thing of the past. In 2026, serverless databases provide infinite scale with zero management.

Pay-as-you-Scale

Modern serverless databases allow developers to focus on data modeling rather than capacity planning. With features like point-in-time recovery and global distribution as standard, the barrier to building high-scale apps has never been lower.

Performance at the Edge

At Anogre, we leverage serverless data layers to ensure our clients' applications are fast, regardless of where their users are located.
