Micro-SaaS for AI Agents

By Anugrah Jacob • April 26, 2026 • 5 min read

Software is no longer just for humans. In 2026, we are building tools designed to be consumed by other AI agents.

The API-First Economy

Micro-SaaS products are emerging that perform one specific task—like data cleaning, image processing, or sentiment analysis—extremely well, specifically for AI agents to integrate into their larger workflows.

Modular Intelligence

At Anogre, we specialize in building these lightweight, high-performance modules that allow AI ecosystems to scale without the bloat of traditional enterprise software.
