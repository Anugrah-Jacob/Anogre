How Flow works

Three ways Flow makes your team faster.

It watches

Flow listens to your live conversations and builds context. It sees what the customer sees, what they've tried, and what's in your help docs.

It suggests

When you're stuck, Flow shows relevant answers, extracts info from your knowledge base, and drafts replies you can edit and send.

It learns

Flow adapts to your team's style. The more you use it, the better it understands your products and support patterns.

What you get

Smart suggestions

Flow suggests the best answers based onsimilar conversations, your docs, and the customer's history.

Auto-context

One click pulls account details, past tickets, usage data, and relevant docs — no tab switching.

Draft replies

Get a drafted reply in your tone. Edit it or write your own — Flow learns either way.

Help your team learn

Flag knowledge gaps. Flow surfaces questions your team struggles with so you can improve docs.

Integrations

Connect to Intercom, Zendesk, Freshdesk, Slack, Notion, and your knowledge base.

Privacy first

Your data is yours. We don't train on your conversations. SOC 2 Type II certified.
